About me

As a student studying POA, I am immersed in a syllabus encompassing a plethora of business-related skill sets. Whilst studying liquidity, profitability and other concepts, I learnt the frameworks involved in analysing a business's performance. This piqued my interest in the sophisticated systems behind business. During lessons, I have had the opportunity to work with technologies and platforms that provide data on businesses. Through these activities, I realised how crucial technology is in the business world, sparking in me the desire to explore more about tech, and more specifically Fintech!

Having a sister who is a graduate of NYP DFI (2021) and a full-time software developer, I have had the chance to look at her past school projects and was awe-struck by how she recreated a bank and made improvements. Having a family which keeps up with finance and business news, I have been immersed in discussions regarding present financial issues (e.g. Inflation and Stagflation)
